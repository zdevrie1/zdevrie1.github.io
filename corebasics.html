Core Basics

What to Focus On First

When starting out, focus on completing missions and progressing through the star chart. This will naturally introduce you to new systems, enemies, and resources. You don’t need to understand everything right away—just keep moving forward and learning as you go.

Understanding Progression

Warframe is built around repetition. You will often need to replay missions multiple times to collect resources, unlock items, or progress further. For many players, this becomes part of the game’s appeal, but it can also feel repetitive at times. Setting several small goals to bounce between and taking breaks can help make progress feel more manageable.

Don’t Try to Learn Everything

Warframe has many systems, including mods, crafting, and different mission types. It’s normal to feel overwhelmed at first. Instead of trying to learn everything at once, focus on one system at a time and return to this guide whenever you need clarification.
