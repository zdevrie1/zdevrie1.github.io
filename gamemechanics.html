Game Mechanics

Movement and Parkour

Warframe movement is faster and more fluid than traditional shooters. Techniques like bullet jumping and sliding allow you to move through missions much faster than simply running.

Moving quickly is also important for survivability. Staying mobile makes it harder for enemies to land hits on you, especially in larger fights where projectiles and enemy numbers increase. Good movement helps you complete missions faster while also keeping you alive more consistently.

Mods and Upgrading

Mods are how you improve both weapons and Warframes. Instead of leveling stats directly, you install mods that increase things like damage, survivability, or utility. Each item has a limited mod capacity, so you can’t equip everything at once and must choose what matters most.

Mods can also be upgraded to become stronger, but stronger mods take up more capacity. This means building effective loadouts is about balance rather than just equipping the most powerful options. Look at the stats of the item you are modding and build around its strengths rather than trying to improve everything at once.

Weapons and Warframes

Warframes are your main playable characters, each with different abilities and playstyles. You start with only a few options, but more Warframes and weapons can be unlocked by defeating bosses, completing missions, or buying their blueprints with credits in the market.

Switching between Warframes and weapons is normal and encouraged. Different missions may be easier with different Warframe abilities or weapon damage types, so experimenting with new gear is part of progression.

Missions and Objectives

Missions are the core of Warframe’s gameplay and come in many different types, such as Exterminate, Defense, and Survival. Each mission has a specific objective that determines how you progress through it.

Before starting a mission, it is helpful to check the objective so you know what to expect. Understanding the goal of each mission type helps prevent confusion during gameplay.

Common Mission Types Objective What to Expect
Spy Locate three terminals, hack into the enemy system, and obtain rewards. Spy missions are mostly about being stealthy. Follow the marker on your screen to the location on the map. There you will find many terminals and defenses that you will need to hack and avoid. Getting caught by defenses will trigger a countdown. If that countdown reaches zero before you hack the final terminal, you will have failed that section and will not get its reward. A certain number of successes are required so try not to fail any!
Survival Keep life support from reaching 0% while keeping yourself alive. Life support will slowly drain over time while a timer counts up. Every 5 minutes you survive without losing life support, you get a reward. If life support reaches 0% before the first 5 minutes, you will fail the mission. If life support runs out anytime after the first 5 minutes, you will stop getting rewards and your health will slowly drain. Keep life support up by finding capsules and towers. Capsules will drop from containers and enemies while towers spawn periodically. Towers will be marked on your map but capsules will only be marked if they are nearby.
Defense Protect a target. Most targets are stationary but some will move around. Protect the target from a number of enemy waves to collect rewards. If at any point the target takes too much damage, you will fail the mission.
Mobile Defense Protect three terminals. This is just like defense except you have to take a datamass to 3 different terminals and defend them for an amount of time while it downloads the data. If any of the terminals take too much damage, you will fail the mission.
Exterminate Kill. It's on sight for anything that moves. This is the simplest mission type. The only way to fail is if you run out of lives.
Assassination You have your target. Take them out. A location is marked on your map. At the location you will find a target—usually a boss—that you need to defeat. Bring them down to complete the mission and claim your reward. The only way to fail is to run out of lives.
Interception Defend terminals from the enemy for a certain time. The enemy is trying to hack terminals to keep the data flowing into their center instead of yours. Defend 4 spots on the map while a progress bar fills to 100%. If the enemy bar reaches 100%, you will fail the mission. Every time your bar reaches 100%, you get a reward.
Capture Capture the enemy target. An enemy will be marked on your map. If you do not deal enough damage to them by the time they reach their extraction, you will fail the mission. Once you have dealt enough damage to them, they will go into a downed state. Interact with them in this state to complete the mission and collect the reward. Make sure not to leave them downed too long. If you wait too long they will bleed out and you will fail the mission.
Rescue Rescue the operative. The location of a captured operative will be marked on the map. Hack into their cell and protect them on your way to extraction. If you are seen by the guards or trigger defenses, you will have a short time before the operative is killed. Stealth will be key but only until you free the target. Once the target is free, the enemy will target both of you. If the target takes too much damage, they will go down. Revive them before they bleed out. Your will fail the mission if they die.
