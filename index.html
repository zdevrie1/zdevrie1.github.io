Welcome, Tenno

This website is a guide for new and returning players of the video game Warframe. If you haven't played before, click here to try it yourself. If you get lost, come back and use this guide to help you find your way again.

If You're New

If you are new to Warframe, don't worry about learning everything at once. Focus on fun first. When you run into something you don’t understand or aren’t sure how to get, come back to this guide for help.

If You're Returning

If you are returning to Warframe after a break, many systems may feel unfamiliar or overwhelming. This guide will help you refresh your understanding and focus on what matters most so you can get back into the game smoothly.
