Resources

Warframe has a large community and many external resources that can help you learn, plan builds, and find specific information. These tools are not required to play the game, but they can make it easier to understand systems and find what you need.

Below are some commonly used resources and when they are most useful.

Warframe Wiki

The Warframe Wiki is one of the most detailed sources of information for the game. It includes drop locations, mission details, and explanations for nearly every system.

This is most useful when you are looking for specific information, such as where to find a resource or how to obtain a certain item.

Visit the Warframe Wiki

Video Guides

Video guides can be helpful for understanding mechanics that are difficult to explain with text alone, such as movement, builds, or mission strategies.

These are especially useful if you prefer seeing how something works rather than reading about it.

Search Warframe Guides on YouTube

Build Guides

Build guides can help you understand how different mods work together and provide examples of effective loadouts.

These are most useful once you have a basic understanding of modding and want to improve your builds or try new setups.

Explore Builds on Overframe

Community and Forums

The Warframe community is active and can be a valuable source of help. Forums, discussions, and player groups can provide answers, advice, and updates on the game.

This is useful when you have specific questions or want to learn from other players’ experiences.

Visit the Warframe Forums

Player Trading

Warframe also includes a player-driven trading system. This allows players to buy and sell items like mods, parts, and blueprints using platinum.

External tools can make trading much easier by showing current prices and active listings. This can save time compared to trading manually in-game.

Trading is not required for progression, but it can help you obtain specific items more quickly if you choose to use it.

Visit Warframe Market
